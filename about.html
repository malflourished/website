About

Motion design, animation, and stop-motion—building worlds frame by frame.

I’m a motion designer and animator with a soft spot for tactile, character-driven work—whether that’s UI systems for a product team or puppets on a stage. This site is a living archive of roles, reels, and experiments; the short version is that I like making things move with intention and a little bit of mischief.

Lately I’ve been focused on brand motion, design systems, and collaborations that sit at the intersection of craft and clarity. Before that, plenty of stop-motion sets, long hours with Dragonframe, and the occasional existential crisis over tie-downs.
